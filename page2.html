The Beast

Everyone is afraid of The Beast. He steals people’s souls and has control over the Woodsman. Throughout the show, he tries to trick Greg and Wirt into following him so that he can show them the way home.

The Woodsman

The Woodsman is a complicated character. At first the boys think that he is evil, but he is actually trying to protect them from The Beast. Without knowing it, though, he is keeping The Beast alive.

Adelaide

Adelaide told Beatrice that she could free her of her curse, but she needed two young boys to help her with “yard work”. When Beatrice brings Wirt and Greg to Adelaide, Adelaide reveals that she wanted to keep the boys as slaves.
