Wirt

Wirt is the older stepbrother of Greg. He is more serious and goal-oriented. Throughout the show, he learns about being a role model for Greg, building empathy, and gaining confidence as a leader.

Greg

Greg is Wirt’s younger stepbrother. He is curious and slightly oblivious to the situation he and his brother are in. Throughout the show, he learns about responsibility and helps Wirt grow as well.

Beatrice

Beatrice is a Bluebird who was once human. She leads the boys to Adelaide, claiming she can “send them home,” but it’s really a trap. Beatrice initially plans to sacrifice the boys to regain her humanity but later changes her mind and helps them escape. Eventually, she still becomes human again.
