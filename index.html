Wirt

Wirt is the older step brother of Greg. He is more serious and goal oriented. Throughout the show, he learns more about being a role model for Greg, build empathy, and confidence as a leader.

alt=<”Wirt”>

Greg

Greg is Wirt’s younger step brother. He is very curious and slightly oblivious to the situation he and his brother is in. Throughout the show, he learns more about responsibility but also helps Wirt learn as well.

Beatrice

Beatrice is a Blue Bird that we later learn was once a human. She leads the boys to Adalaide so that she can “send them home” but it was really a trap, but Beatrice knew and was willing to sacrifice the boys to make her human again. She later changes her mind and helps the boys escape Adalaide and still becomes human again.
